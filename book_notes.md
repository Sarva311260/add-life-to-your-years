# Book Notes: Health, Wellness and Vitality

## Title & Subtitle
- "Add Life to Your Years: A Guide to Health, Wellness, and Vitality"

## Structure
- Part 1: Brief Overview of How Our Body Works (cells, microbiome, communication)
- Part 2: Identifying Challenges to Wellbeing (8 categories)
- Part 3: Solutions for Health, Wellness, and Vitality

## 8 Challenge Categories (from the book)
1. Lifestyle
2. Environmental Factors
3. Genetic Make-up
4. Structural Conditions
5. Stress Levels
6. Lack of Purpose or Direction
7. Absence of Meaningful Relationships
8. Physical trauma or defect

## Key Themes
- Healthspan vs lifespan
- 30-37 trillion cells, 50% human / 50% microbial
- Holobiont concept - we are ecological units
- Whole food plant-based, low fat approach
- Cell health = organ health = body health
- Biochemical + electrical communication
- Microbiome importance (gut-brain axis, serotonin production)
- Self-repair and neuroplasticity
- Multifaceted causes that GPs can't address in 15-20 min consultations
